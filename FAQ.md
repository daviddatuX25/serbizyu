# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This document answers common questions about the Serbizyu platform, its features, and how to use it effectively.

---

##  G - General Questions

#### Q1: What is Serbizyu?
**A:** Serbizyu is a local services marketplace designed to connect service providers (Sellers) with customers (Buyers) in the Philippines. It facilitates the discovery, booking, and management of services through a structured and interactive platform.

#### Q2: Who can use Serbizyu?
**A:** Anyone in need of a service (a Buyer) or anyone looking to offer their skills and services (a Seller) can use the platform.

#### Q3: Is the platform free to use?
**A:** Yes, signing up and browsing services is free for all users. We charge a small platform fee on completed transactions, which is deducted from the seller's earnings.

#### Q4: What kind of services can I find on Serbizyu?
**A:** The platform supports a wide range of local services. Categories are managed by the platform, ensuring a structured and easy-to-navigate marketplace for everything from home repairs and cleaning to creative and digital services.

## B - For Service Seekers (Buyers)

#### Q1: How do I find a service?
**A:** You can use the "Browse" page to search for services. You can filter by category, location, price range, and keywords to find exactly what you need.

#### Q2: What are the different ways to book a service?
**A:** You have three main ways to get a service:
1.  **Book a Service:** Find a pre-defined service listing from a seller and book it directly.
2.  **Create an Open Offer:** Post a public request for a service you need, detailing your budget and requirements. Sellers can then bid on your offer.
3.  **Use Quick Deals:** Scan a seller's QR code to start a real-time session to negotiate and book one of their services instantly.

#### Q3: How do I pay for a service?
**A:** The platform supports simulated payments through Xendit (for demo purposes), which includes methods like GCash and Credit Card. Some services may also offer a "Pay After" option, where you pay after the service workflow is complete. Sellers can also confirm cash payments for in-person transactions.

#### Q4: What happens after I book an order?
**A:** A new order is created, and a dedicated "Work Instance" page becomes available. Here, you can track the progress of the service through a step-by-step workflow defined by the seller, communicate with the seller, share files, and see real-time updates.

## S - For Service Providers (Sellers)

#### Q1: How do I start selling my services?
**A:** After creating an account, you can go to your Creator Dashboard to create your first "Service" listing. You'll need to provide details like title, description, price, and define the steps (workflow) required to complete your service.

#### Q2: What is a Workflow Template?
**A:** A Workflow Template is a reusable set of steps that defines how you complete a service. For example, a "Graphic Design" workflow might have steps like "Initial Consultation," "Draft Submission," and "Final Revisions." You attach a workflow to each of your services, ensuring a consistent process for every order.

#### Q3: How can I get booked by clients?
**A:** Clients can find you in three ways:
1.  **Direct Booking:** They can book one of your published service listings.
2.  **Bidding on Open Offers:** You can browse "Open Offers" posted by buyers and place a bid on projects that match your skills.
3.  **Quick Deals:** You can create a unique QR code for your services. When a potential client scans it, they can instantly start a session with you to book a service.

#### Q4: How do I get paid?
**A:** When an order is completed and paid for by the buyer, the funds (minus the platform fee) are added to your account. Payouts are processed through a disbursement system, simulating a transfer to your registered bank account. For cash transactions, you confirm the payment directly.

**A:** Yes, you can upload multiple images to your Service and Open Offer listings to provide more detail and attract more customers. Video uploads are a planned future feature.

#### Q3: What does "Pay First Required" mean?
**A:** When creating a service, a seller can toggle the "Pay First" option. If it's on, the buyer must complete payment before the seller can start the work. If it's off, the work can begin immediately, and payment is requested upon completion of the service workflow.

## üí∏ Open Offers & Bidding

#### Q1: How do I create an Open Offer?
**A:** As a buyer, you can navigate to the "Open Offers" section and create a new offer by detailing your requirements, timeline, and budget.

#### Q2: How does bidding work?
**A:** As a seller, you can browse Open Offers and place a bid on any that you are qualified for. Your bid will include your proposed price and a reference to one of your existing services (to determine the workflow).

#### Q3: How do I accept a bid?
**A:** The buyer who created the Open Offer can review all bids they receive. When they accept a bid, a formal "Order" is automatically created based on the bid's terms, and the workflow begins.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Orders & Workflows

#### Q1: What is a "Work Instance"?
**A:** A Work Instance is the execution environment for an order. It's where you track the step-by-step progress of the service, from start to finish.

#### Q2: What is an "Activity Thread"?
**A:** Within a work instance, sellers and buyers can create Activity Threads to communicate. These can be simple updates, questions, requests for evidence (like photos of completed work), or reports of an issue. Other users can then reply to these threads, creating a conversation around a specific topic.

#### Q3: What are "Strict Prompts"?
**A:** A seller can add a "Strict Prompt" (a required question) to a workflow step. This blocks the workflow from progressing to the next step until the designated person (either the buyer or the seller) provides an answer. This is useful for getting mandatory information before continuing.

## üì± Quick Deals (QR Code)

#### Q1: What is a Quick Deal?
**A:** A Quick Deal is a feature that allows sellers to generate a unique QR code. When a customer scans this code, it initiates a real-time "session" where they can browse the seller's services and propose a deal on the spot.

#### Q2: What are the different Quick Deal modes?
**A:**
*   **Two-Way Mode (Default):** The most secure mode. When a customer scans the QR code, the seller must first approve the session before the customer can see the services. This prevents spam.
*   **One-Way Mode:** The customer can immediately see the services and start making proposals after scanning. This is faster but carries a higher risk of spam.

#### Q3: What happens when a proposal is accepted?
**A:** When one party accepts a proposal made within a Quick Deal session, an official "Order" is created instantly, and both users are redirected to the order page to begin the workflow.

## üí≥ Payments & Payouts

#### Q1: How are payments handled?
**A:** The platform uses a simulated payment gateway (Xendit Demo) to handle online transactions. When a buyer pays, the funds are held in a simulated escrow until certain conditions are met (e.g., work completion).

#### Q2: What is a "Disbursement"?
**A:** A disbursement is the process of transferring a seller's earnings from their Serbizyu account to their personal bank account. This is scheduled automatically after an order is successfully completed and the clearing period has passed.

#### Q3: Is there a platform fee?
**A:** Yes, a small percentage-based platform fee is deducted from the total order amount. The fee is configurable and is used to maintain and improve the platform.

## üí¨ Messaging

#### Q1: How many messaging systems are there?
**A:** There are three distinct communication systems:
1.  **Direct Messages:** For private, one-on-one conversations between any two users.
2.  **Contextual Threads:** For public or semi-private discussions attached to a specific item, like a Service or Open Offer listing.
3.  **Activity Threads:** For private communication between the buyer and seller within a specific order's work instance.

#### Q1: How do I manage my notification preferences?
**A:** In your profile settings, you can find a "Notification Preferences" section where you can enable or disable different types of notifications (e.g., new messages, bid alerts) for different channels (e.g., Email, In-App).

#### Q2: Can I add multiple addresses?
**A:** Yes, you can add multiple addresses to your profile and set one as your primary address. This is useful for specifying different locations for service delivery or pickup.

## üõ°Ô∏è Safety & Trust

#### Q1: How does Serbizyu handle disputes?
**A:** The platform has a reporting system where users can "flag" content or users for review by administrators. This includes flagging services, open offers, and even orders for issues like spam, fraud, or inappropriate content.

#### Q2: What is Escrow?
**A:** For online payments, the buyer's payment is held in a secure reserve (escrow). The funds are only released to the seller after the work is completed, providing a layer of protection for both parties.
