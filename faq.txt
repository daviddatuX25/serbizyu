Serbizyu is a local services marketplace connecting customers with service providers in the Philippines. Find services you need or offer your skills to earn.Who can use Serbizyu?
Anyone can use Serbizyu. You can be a customer looking for services, a provider offering your skills, or both at the same time.Is it free to use?
Yes, signing up and browsing is completely free. We only charge a small platform fee on completed transactions.What areas do you cover?
Currently focused on the Philippines, with plans to expand to more locations.ACCOUNT & PROFILEHow do I create an account?

An Open Offer is when a customer posts what they need and service providers bid on it. The customer then chooses the best bid.How do I create an Open Offer?

Click "Post Open Offer"
Describe what you need
Set your budget
Choose category
Optionally add a workflow
Wait for service providers to bid
Is workflow required for Open Offers?
No, workflows are optional for Open Offers. You can add one if you have specific steps in mind, but it's not required.How do I bid on an Open Offer?

Browse Open Offers
Click on one that matches your skills
Click "Place Bid"
Select which of your services you'll use
Set your price

How many bids can an Open Offer receive?
The creator sets a maximum (usually 5-10 bids). Once reached, the offer closes.What happens when my bid is accepted?
An order is automatically created. You'll be notified and can start working based on the payment terms.ORDERS & WORKFLOWWhat is an Order?
An order is a confirmed job. It's created when:

Someone books your service directly
A customer accepts your bid on an Open Offer
A Quick Deal proposal is accepted (coming soon)
What are the order statuses?

Pending Payment: Waiting for customer payment
Confirmed: Paid and ready to start work
In Progress: Work is happening
Awaiting Payment: Work done, waiting for payment (if Pay After)
Completed: Everything finished, payment released
Cancelled: Order was cancelled
What is workflow execution?
Workflow is the step-by-step process of completing the work. Each step might involve:

Posting updates with photos/videos
Asking questions (prompts)
Getting customer approval
Moving to the next phase
What are Activity Threads?
Updates you post during work. You can share:

Progress photos
Video updates
Written comments
Evidence of completion
What are Prompts?
Questions asked during the work process. Example: "What color would you like?" or "Should I proceed with Option A or B?"What is a Strict Prompt?
A question that MUST be answered before work can continue. The "Complete & Continue" button stays disabled until answered.Who can move the workflow forward?
Only the service provider (seller) can click "Complete & Continue" to progress to the next step.Can I skip a workflow step?
Service providers can skip steps with a valid reason. The skip is logged and visible to the customer.Can I message during an order?
Yes. Every order has a built-in messaging thread where buyer and seller can communicate.PAYMENTSHow can customers pay?
Two payment methods:

Online: GCash, Credit Card, Bank Transfer (via Xendit)
Cash: In-person payment (seller confirms receipt)
Is online payment safe?
Yes. Payments are processed by Xendit, a licensed payment gateway. Money is held in escrow until work is completed.What is escrow?
Money is held securely by the platform. It's only released to the seller after:

Work is completed
Customer is satisfied
Waiting period passes (usually 3 days)
When do sellers receive their money?

Pay First orders: 3 days after work completion
Pay After orders: 3 days after customer payment
